Positive Discipline Parenting

Kind and firm parenting that raises capable, responsible children — bilingual, self-paced, practical.

Based on the work of Jane Nelsen and Rudolf Dreikurs, this course walks you through ten practical modules — from understanding why children misbehave, to building routines, encouragement, and family meetings that last a lifetime. Every module includes a real-life example, the common mistake parents make, how to handle it instead, and one suggestion to improve.
